THIS WEBSITE

is designed for people who are over 50 years old and want to get information about Myeongji in Korea. Through this web page, they will be able to get information on famous cafes and restaurants in Myongji. In particular, cafes and restaurants with beautiful scenery and luxurious interiors that take advantage of the characteristics of Myeongji surrounded by the sea were selected mainly. As a web page considering the elderly, we made the page as easy and simple as possible, and minimized the difficulty when using the web page with large letters and an intuitive interface.
